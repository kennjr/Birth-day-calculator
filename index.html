About

This is a simple site that lets you find out the actual day of the week when you were born.
It then gives you what your Akan name would be. Akan names are names given to Ghanaians based on the day they were born.
Interesting fact : based on this naming convention the first president of Ghana Kwame Nkrumah was born on a Saturday.

Fill in the form to find out what your Akan name would be right now!!
